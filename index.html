This project allows you to mix together tracks like a DJ.

I created it during my time at the App Academy bootcamp in 2020.

You can peruse the code on my GitHub.

If you like what you see, feel free to message me through my LinkedIn.

1. Drag a record from the crate in the middle onto the "A" turntable.

2. Press play and mess around with the panning or the speed.

3. Drag another record onto the "B" turntable.

4. Use the slider in the middle to mix the sounds!
